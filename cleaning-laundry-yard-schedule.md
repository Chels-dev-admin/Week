# Cleaning, Laundry & Yard — Optimized Weekly Schedule

Built on your operating system's rules: **office days stay near-zero**, home days (Tue/Thu) and weekends carry the load, **everything is time-boxed** (when the timer ends, it's done — good enough is good enough), and **one zone at a time** so there's never a giant undifferentiated to-do list.

The whole week touches every zone once, spreads 3–4 laundry loads across days you're home to manage them, and puts yard work on Saturday where it doubles as movement.

---

## The week at a glance

| Day | Type | Indoor | Laundry | Yard |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | Office | 10-min reset only | — | 2-min pass · **bins out tonight** |
| **Tue** | Home | **Kitchen** (15 min) | Load 1 — clothes | 2-min pass |
| **Wed** | Office | 10-min reset only | — | 2-min pass |
| **Thu** | Home | **Bathroom** (15 min) | Load 2 — darks/clothes | 2-min pass |
| **Fri** | Office | 10-min reset only | — | 2-min pass |
| **Sat** | Weekend | **Floors + pet areas** | Load 3 — towels | **Yard block** (30–45 min) |
| **Sun** | Weekend | **Bedroom + living** | Load 4 — bed sheets | 2-min pass |

Each of your four zones gets one proper session a week, and the daily reset stops anything piling up in between.

---

## Daily anchors (every day)

- **10-minute reset (timer):** kitchen surfaces wiped, dishes in/out, quick tidy of the room you're in. Stop when it rings.
- **2-minute dog-waste pass:** fold it into the evening dog care you already do — quick, keeps the yard on top of two dogs.

## Office days (Mon / Wed / Fri) — protected

Just the daily reset and the 2-minute waste pass. **No zone, no line-drying.** If a load is genuinely urgent, dryer only — never hang washing on an office day, because no one's home to rescue it from rain. Folding, if needed, is a calm wind-down task, not a chore block.

## Tuesday & Thursday (home days) — one zone + one load

- **Tue — Kitchen:** benches, stovetop, sink, mop if time. 15-min timer.
- **Thu — Bathroom:** toilet, shower, sink, floor. 15-min timer.
- **Laundry:** start the load in your morning reclaimed block. Fine day → hang it out, bring in after work. Wet → dryer. (See the weather rule below.)

## Saturday — outdoor + deeper indoor + towels

- **Yard block (30–45 min, counts as your strength/cardio):** thorough dog-waste sweep, leaves / paths / patio tidy. **Mow & edge on alternate Saturdays** (see seasonal dial). *(Bins go out Monday night — see below.)*
- **Indoor — Floors + pet areas:** vacuum and mop through the house, wash the dog beds, wipe the feeding zone and around the litter.
- **Laundry — towels:** line-dry in the weekend daylight, or dryer.

## Sunday — bedroom/living + sheets

- **Zone — Bedroom + living:** dust, clear surfaces, vacuum.
- **Bed sheets:** strip, wash, and remake the same day (line-dry if fine).
- **During your Sunday reset:** glance at the week's forecast and pre-decide line vs. dryer for Tue/Thu — one less decision midweek.

---

## Bins — Monday nights

Put the bins out **Monday evening** (when you're home from the office). **Red (general waste) goes out every week.** The second bin alternates fortnightly:

| Monday | Bins out |
|---|---|
| Jul 6 | 🔴 Red + 🟡 Yellow (recycling) |
| Jul 13 | 🔴 Red + 🟢 Green (garden waste) |
| Jul 20 | 🔴 Red + 🟡 Yellow |
| Jul 27 | 🔴 Red + 🟢 Green |

…and so on, alternating yellow / green each week. A 2-minute job — fold it into your evening dog care. (Calendar reminders for this are set up separately.)

---

## The weather rule (line-dry + dryer)

A simple default so it's never an open question:

- **Fine forecast + you're home (Tue/Thu/weekend):** line-dry.
- **Wet, or unsure:** dryer.
- **Office day (Mon/Wed/Fri):** dryer, always — no line-drying when you're out 6am–6pm.

Deciding Tue/Thu's method on Sunday removes the daily "will it rain?" loop.

---

## Seasonal dial — the one thing that flexes

Only the **Saturday mow** changes with the seasons; everything else stays put:

- **Spring / summer (fast growth):** mow **every** Saturday.
- **Shoulder seasons:** **every other** Saturday.
- **Winter (slow):** roughly **monthly**.

On non-mow Saturdays the yard block is just the waste sweep + tidy + bins (~15 min).

---

## Guardrails (same as the rest of your system)

- **Time-box everything.** When the timer ends, the zone is done — even if it's only "good enough." That's the OCD guardrail: the timer decides, not the urge to keep going.
- **One zone at a time.** Never let it become a whole-house to-do list.
- **Overloaded? Drop in this order:** deep/zone clean first → a laundry load can wait a day → yard tidy — **except the dog-waste pass**, which stays. Never cut sleep, animal care, or decompression.
- **A missed zone rolls forward** to its next natural slot. You don't cram two weeks of bathroom into one session.

**Zones:** Tue kitchen · Thu bathroom · Sat floors+pets · Sun bedroom+living · daily 10-min reset
**Laundry (4):** Tue clothes · Thu darks · Sat towels · Sun sheets

**Bins (Mon night):** 🔴 red weekly · 🟡 yellow & 🟢 green alternate fortnightly
