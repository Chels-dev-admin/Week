# Curly Hair (3a–3c) — Maintenance Schedule

Built for **washing every 2–3 days**, slotted into your week so wash days land on your **non-office days** (where you have time to style) and office mornings stay a quick 5-minute refresh. Same spirit as the rest of your system: consistent anchors, low decision-load, forgiving when a day slips.

---

## The weekly rhythm

| Day | Type | Hair |
|---|---|---|
| **Sun** | Weekend | **WASH** + deep-conditioning mask + style |
| Mon | Office | Refresh — spritz + scrunch |
| **Tue** | Home | **WASH** + style |
| Wed | Office | Refresh |
| **Thu** | Home | **WASH** + style |
| Fri | Office | Refresh |
| Sat | Weekend | Refresh |

**Every night:** pineapple + satin bonnet (or silk pillowcase).

Three washes a week (Sun / Tue / Thu), spaced every 2–3 days, all on days you're not rushing out the door.

---

## Wash day (Sun / Tue / Thu)

1. **Cleanse** — sulfate-free shampoo or co-wash, focus on the scalp.
2. **Condition** — a rich, slippy conditioner; finger-detangle in the shower while it's in.
3. **Sunday extra** — swap in a **deep-conditioning mask** (5–15 min; warm with a shower cap if you can).
4. **Style on soaking-wet hair** — leave-in → curl cream → gel. Scrunch upward; don't rake the cast out.
5. **Dry** — air-dry or diffuse on low heat. Once fully dry, scrunch out the crunch (SOTC) for soft, defined curls.

## Refresh day (Mon / Wed / Fri / Sat)

Take down the pineapple, mist with water (or a water + leave-in mix), scrunch a little gel or cream into the flattest sections, and go. **3–5 minutes** — this is why office mornings stay easy.

## Protective sleep (every night)

Pineapple (loose, high, loose tie) + satin bonnet/scarf, or a silk/satin pillowcase. Protects the curl cast, cuts frizz and breakage. It's folded into your existing wind-down step.

## Monthly — protein treatment

Once a month, use a **protein treatment** on the Sunday wash **instead of** the plain mask, then follow with a moisturising conditioner. Protein restores strength and elasticity; too much without moisture makes hair brittle, so keep it to monthly and always chase it with moisture.

## Every ~8–10 weeks — trim

A light dusting/trim keeps ends healthy and curls springy. The reminder repeats every ~10 weeks — move it to suit your growth.

---

## Product-type cheat sheet

- **Wash:** sulfate-free shampoo / co-wash
- **Condition:** rich, slippy conditioner
- **Weekly:** deep-conditioning mask
- **Monthly:** protein treatment (then moisture)
- **Style:** leave-in + curl cream + gel (for hold and cast)
- **Refresh:** water / leave-in spray + a little gel
- **Sleep:** satin bonnet or silk pillowcase

---

## How it fits your system

- Wash days sit on **Sun / Tue / Thu** — your days with time — so office mornings stay a quick refresh.
- The nightly bonnet folds into the wind-down you already do.
- Same guardrail: if a wash day gets cut, just **refresh and roll the wash forward** — curls forgive a delayed wash far better than a rushed one.
- All of this is now in your app's day plans, and calendar reminders are set for wash days, the monthly protein treatment, and the trim.
